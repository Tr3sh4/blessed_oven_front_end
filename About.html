About Blessed Oven

Blessed Oven began as a small home-based pastry shop in Pasig City, lovingly run by a passionate baker who turned her dreams into reality. Over time, the business grew from selling cookies and cakes to becoming a beloved local spot offering tapsilog meals, chicken wings, and signature shakes.

Our mission is to deliver delicious and freshly made food to our customers, whether you're craving sweet treats or savory meals. At Blessed Oven, we believe in quality, warmth, and community. Every product is made with love, ensuring that each bite feels like home.

We are continuously improving our service and expanding our menu to meet the evolving tastes of our customers. Thank you for making us part of your everyday celebrations and cravings!
